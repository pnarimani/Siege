# Orders Summary

Orders are one time special actions that the player can take during the day phase. 
They represent strategic decisions that can have significant impacts on the city's resources, morale, and survival chances. 
Each order has specific conditions for enactment, a cooldown period before it can be used again, and a narrative description that adds flavor to the consequences of the action.
Orders execute immediately and don't take multiple days like missions. They can be used to manage crises, boost morale, or make tough choices that affect the city's trajectory through the siege.

Orders can only be executed during day phase.
